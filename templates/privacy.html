Privacy-first design

Your reflections are yours. This app is intentionally built with minimal data handling.

What we collect

  • Journal text: sent to process your request and generate insights.
  • Technical logs: basic server logs for reliability (no journal content).

How your data is used

  • Not logged in: entries are processed and discarded.
  • Logged in: if you use the feature, entries are stored in your account and can be deleted by you.
  • No ads, no third‑party analytics, and no user tracking.

Your choices

  • Use a nickname; avoid sharing personal details in journal text.
  • You may clear your browser history and cache after use.

Security

  • Transport security is recommended when deployed (HTTPS).
  • No accounts or databases are used in this demo to reduce risk.

Wellness notice: This is not medical advice. If you are in crisis, contact local emergency services or a trusted adult immediately.
